FAQ

Everything you need to know.

What is the dress code?

We invite you to dress in semi-formal attire.

Are children invited?

While we love your little ones, our celebration is an adult-only affair (ages 12 and older).
We hope you take this opportunity to enjoy a night off and celebrate with us!

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony will take place outdoors in the Tapestry House gardens. The reception will follow indoors. Please bring a light layer, as Colorado evenings can get quite cool once the sun sets.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

While we have hired a wonderful photographer to capture the magic, please feel free to take as many photos as you like!
