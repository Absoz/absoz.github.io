Arrival & Lodging

Wedding Location:

The Tapestry House
3212 N Overland Trail, Laporte, CO 80535

Traveler's Note: When using GPS, please ensure you enter the city as Laporte. Some systems may misroute you if "Fort Collins" is used.

From I-25: Take Exit 269B (Hwy 14/Mulberry St) West. Follow Mulberry to Riverside Ave, turn right. Continue to College Ave, turn right, then follow signs for CR 54G to N Overland Trail.
